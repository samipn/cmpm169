Experiment 7 - Data Visualization & Networks

Imitate

I decided I wanted to do easy to read data visualization. So I decided to look on openprocessing and found a lot of cool examples of complex ways to represnt data, but then I found some good simple ones too.

Integrate

Innovate

For this step, I decided to make it show local data of Santa Cruz weather, and show the highest temperatures recorded in 2023 for each month.
