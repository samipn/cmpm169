Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I started with a sphere, and then I branched out, by using curves and lines to see what I could make. I also found a lot of cool 3D art with landscapes and even ducks.

Integrate

To integrate, I took what I saw, and made it mesh together. First was the sphere and curves, then the math lines came in the end.

Innovate

For the final product, I combined all the parts to make everything fit well. And now you can move around the sphere and scroll in and out to zoom and see a different perspective.
