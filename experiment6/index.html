Experiment 6 - Grammars & Text Art

Imitate

For this step, I went ahead and looked for cool text art where the text was animated and able to be interacted with. I found many cool projects to help me acheive this.

Integrate

To integrate, I went through the text art and found stuff that would work well with each other and ultimately decided on a cut off effect.

Innovate

I used that cut off effect to make something that looks cool, while also messing around with the radius of the circle and how the colors change.
